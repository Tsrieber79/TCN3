Product Description

SuperSep™ is a high performance filter aid designed to compete with products made from diatomaceous earth, perlite and cellulose. Its dead end pore structure and unique shape create a low tortuosity filtration environment that is efficient both in energy consumption and solid liquid separation. SuperSep™ features high density filter cakes for efficient use of confined spaces in pressure leaf filters and recessed chamber presses. Its dead end pore structure imparts high capillary pressure for solids containment and excellent filter cake release. SuperSep™ is available in a number of dust controlled grades.

Product Details

  • Low tortuosity
  • High density cakes
  • Natural material
  • Dust control available
