About TCN Technologies

Founded in 2002, TCN Technologies is a family owned business which capitalizes on 15 years of filtration technology development and operations. Innovative product development and aggressive customer service have been the hallmark of the company. The company’s functional and cost effective product line provides exceptional value to its customers.

TCN’s products can be found in process filtration and waste treatment operations at customer sites in the US and abroad. TCN manufactures its products at two facilities in the US which are strategically located to provide excellent transportation value.

TCN’s client list is diverse and strong including, DuPont, Massachusetts General Hospital, Harley-Davison, Honeywell, Wahl Clippers, Dow, Kawasaki, Gilbarco-Veeder Root and many others.
